Hack The Kernel

ops-class.org includes everything you need to learn about operating systems online.

Learn

ops-class.org includes slides, hundreds of hours of videotaped lectures, and sample exams: everything you need to learn OS concepts online at your own pace.

Hack

Challenging programming assignments give you the chance to implement your own OS. Try your hand at the same assignments that Mark Zuckerberg struggled with!

Compete

Think your VM system is the fastest? Find out! Our test161 tool allows you to evaluate your solutions and compete with other students.

Let's get started!

Watch the introductory lecture or get started on ASST0. Good luck, and have fun.
