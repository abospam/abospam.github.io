== Review: Three Goals

[.slider]
.What are the three goals Lampson focuses on?
* Functionality
* Speed
* Fault-tolerance

[.slider]
.What are the three parts of the design task he identifies?
* Ensuring completeness
* Choosing interfaces
* Designing implementations
